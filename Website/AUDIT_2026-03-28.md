### Photo sourcing status

Extensive automated search was performed for publicly available photos for all 7 brands. No usable photos were found:

- **Web search** (25+ queries, Spanish and English): zero results for all brand names
- **Instagram**: login walls, forbidden errors, or 0 posts on all profiles
- **Google Images**: blocked by CAPTCHA/anti-bot detection
- **Facebook**: requires authentication
- **PedidosYa**: generic restaurant lists, not brand-specific
- **TripAdvisor**: wrong city redirect (Salvador, Brazil instead of Asuncion)

**Next step**: User will provide photos manually from internal Omniprise assets or Instagram app downloads. When photos are available, place `.webp` files in `/public/brands/gallery/{slug}/` (e.g., `1.webp`, `2.webp`) and populate `galleryImages` arrays in `brands.ts`.
